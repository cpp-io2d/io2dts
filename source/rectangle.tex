\rSec1 [\iotwod.rectangle.intro] {\tcode{rectangle} Description}

\pnum
\indexlibrary{\idxcode{rectangle}}%
The class \tcode{rectangle} describes an object that represents a rectangle, storing a two-dimensional coordinate representing the upper left corner of a rectangle, and the rectangle's width and height.
