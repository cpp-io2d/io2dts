\rSec1 [\iotwod.arc.intro] {\tcode{arc} Description}

\pnum
\indexlibrary{\idxcode{arc}}
The class \tcode{arc} describes a \tcode{path} operation. For a description of its meaning within a \tcode{path}, see the meaning of \tcode{path_data_type::arc} in Table~\ref{tab:\iotwod.pathdatatype.meanings}.
